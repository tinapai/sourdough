Meet the Baker

Welcome to my cottage bakery! I'm Tina. What started as a craving for good bread and a bit of sourdough starter from a friend turned into a passion for the sourdough craft and a beautiful way to share and connect with others. It brings me joy when people say they love my bread, and I am always striving to hone my technique to make the best bread that I can. My mission with this bakery is to provide quality fresh bread made with simple ingredients to my local community.
